How Many People Can Our PA System Cover?

The coverage of our PA system depends on how it's being used and the surrounding environment. Below are general estimates:

For Background Music (Minimal Speech Clarity Needed):

Indoors: Up to 200 people

Outdoors: Up to 100 people

For Clear Speaking & Announcements:

Indoors (Quiet Environment – Conferences, Meetings, Churches): Up to 125 people

Indoors (Noisy Environment – Parties, Weddings, Live Music Venues): Up to 75 people

Outdoors (Quiet Environment – Speeches, Ceremonies, Community Gatherings): Up to 150 people

Outdoors (Noisy Environment – Street Performances, Markets, Festivals): Up to 75 people

If you're unsure whether our system meets your needs, feel free to email us for guidance!

What do we need?

The main thing we require for your event to run smoothly is working plug sockets within a maximum of 10m from where you want the PA System.
